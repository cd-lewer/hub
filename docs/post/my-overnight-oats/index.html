Overnight Oats

May 30, 2025
2 minute read

Quick and Easy Breakfast

The best way to start your day is with a dense nutrient bomb at the beginning of the day. Something with plenty of healthy fats, fiber, antioxidants, and slow-digesting carbohydrates — all working together to fuel your body and mind.

This overnight oat blend is more than just convenient — it’s a powerful, balanced meal designed to nourish your gut, support your brain, and stabilize your energy levels throughout the day. It’s packed with omega-3s from walnuts and chia seeds, rich in fiber and resistant starch from oats, and loaded with anti-inflammatory compounds like turmeric and blueberries. The combination of protein, fat, and complex carbs helps prevent blood sugar spikes and crashes, keeping you fuller longer and helping you stay sharp and focused.

Whether you’re lifting weights, walking into meetings, or chasing dreams, this breakfast sets you up to thrive.

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Why It’s Included
Protein Oats ½ cup High-protein whole grain carbs for long-lasting energy and fullness
Chia Seeds 1 tbsp Omega-3s, fiber, and a gelatinous texture that aids digestion
Pumpkin Seeds 1 tbsp Rich in magnesium, zinc, and healthy fats for heart and immune support
Walnuts 1 tbsp (crushed) Plant-based omega-3s and polyphenols to reduce inflammation
Blueberries ¼ cup Antioxidants to fight oxidative stress and boost brain health
Coconut Flakes 1 tbsp Healthy fats and texture; supports metabolism and adds flavor
PB2 (Powdered Peanut Butter) 1 tbsp Protein and flavor with less fat and fewer calories than traditional peanut butter
Turmeric ¼ tsp Anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties
Black Pepper A pinch Enhances absorption of curcumin in turmeric
Oat Milk ½ cup Dairy-free creamy base with added fiber
Maple Syrup 1 tsp (optional) Natural sweetener with trace minerals — better than refined sugar
